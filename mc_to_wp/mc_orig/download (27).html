Monthly gathering for Riverside community
View this email in your browser

Dear Riverside resident,

Following on from the successful (and very wet!) Coronation Street Party we are excited to announce a new community idea. We would like to invite you to a monthly gathering on the first Sunday of each month at the Chequers
(the green space at the top of Priory Road). We will meet at 4pm, weather allowing. Please bring yourselves, your children and neighbours for a brief get together - about 45 minutes is the plan. Please bring some snacks and something to drink. We will all share.

The first one will be on 4th June from 4pm.

If you would like to join the Riverside community WhatsApp group, please use the QR code below, or send a message to Olympia (07341 893825).

If you would like to help to organise the meet ups, please let us know!

We look forward to seeing you there!
The Riverside Area Residents’ Association
