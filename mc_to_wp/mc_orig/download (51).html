1st December 2020


Dear All,

 

This is a brief message to update you on the easyHotel campaign, and on our wider RARA arrangements.
 

Firstly, on the easyHotel campaign, my email in November flagged we were waiting for a court date for an oral hearing. The oral hearing will decide whether the court will allow our claim to go forward to be considered at a full hearing. We had expected the oral hearing to take place in December, but we now know this is expected to be scheduled in the New Year, mid-January at the earliest. I will, of course, keep you posted.

 

Secondly, we are continuing our fundraising efforts for the easyHotel campaign. Thank you again to those who have contributed to date. We do, however, need to continue to work hard to raise funds to continue the fight. If you would feel able to help please do get in touch with me as Treasurer or visit our Crowdfunder page (active until the 4th December).

 

Finally, on RARA arrangements, thank you again to all residents who have volunteered to support RARA. At our RARA meeting for residents on 20th October (via Zoom) my role as Treasurer to the association was confirmed. We were also delighted to welcome Sarah Mann to the team. Sarah has recently taken on the role of revamping our communications, including introducing a new Facebook page (RiversideCambs), Twitter account (@RiversideCambs) and above mentioned Crowdfunder page. Sarah also designed the RARA leaflet which was distributed to over 800 homes in October. Sarah has redesigned our Supporters Updates which remain a core and direct method of communication with over 150 supporters (and the number is growing!) Please do keep ‘spreading the word’ so we can keep that core list growing, and our community updated and informed.

 

At the time of the October meeting we were still seeking a volunteer(s) for the role of RARA Secretary. I am pleased to confirm that Suzanne German has kindly agreed to take on the role of Secretary and we are delighted to welcome her aboard. One of Suzanne’s early roles will be to get in touch and liaise with street rep volunteers and campaign co-ordinators, so if you have volunteered as a street rep watch this space. As soon as possible the RARA website can then be updated with names and contact details.

 

If you have any questions or queries do, of course, please do let me know.

 

Kind Regards,

Mike Evans
easyHotel Campaign Co-ordinator for RARA
