Supporters update

Planning application to improve facilities at the Engineer's House

The Museum of Technology has submitted a planning application to:

  • replace the existing temporary Engineer's House bar and servery with a single "more unified" permanent structure
  • improve accessibility through a new outdoors stair with platform lift
  • upgrade the existing disabled WC.
The closing date for comments is 14 February 2024.

To access the application:
  • Follow the direct link https://applications.greatercambridgeplanning.org/online-applications/applicationDetails.do?activeTab=summary&keyVal=S60CKCDXJ5F00
  • Alternatively, visit the city's planning portal (https://applications.greatercambridgeplanning.org/online-applications/) and enter the reference number 23/04873/FUL


Kind regards,
