Supporters update

Community orchard session, Wed 7th August

Please see below a message from Abbey People:


Could you join us tomorrow morning at the Margaret Wright Community Orchard for a summer gardening session? We’ll be there 9:30am – 11:30am, before it gets too hot 🌞 It would be brilliant to have your help!

The Community Orchard is on Whitehill Road, CB5 8LZ (what3words ///bath.mixer.sleep). The no.3 bus stops on Whitehill Road and then it’s a short walk to the Orchard. Please dress appropriately for the weather, we suggest wearing comfortable sturdy shoes and clothes that you don’t mind getting dirty. Bring a water bottle and a hat if it’s sunny.


Kind regards,
The RARA steering committee
